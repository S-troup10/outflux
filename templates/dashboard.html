Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2025

At Outflux, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your personal information and explains your rights in relation to it.

1. What We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, authentication method (e.g., Google), and account preferences.
  • Usage Data: Logs of app activity, features accessed, and settings interactions.
  • Payment Data: Managed securely via Stripe. Outflux never stores raw card numbers.
  • Support Inquiries: Messages or emails you send us when contacting support.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate and improve Outflux's features and interface.
  • To send essential service updates or billing-related emails.
  • To provide user support and help resolve issues.
  • To enforce terms of service or prevent abuse and fraud.

3. Data Security & Storage

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.
  • We use Supabase for secure backend and database management.
  • Access to your data is limited to authorized personnel only.

4. Cookies & Tracking

  • We use essential cookies to manage login sessions and preferences.
  • No third-party marketing or advertising cookies are used.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google: For secure login authentication.
  • Stripe: For managing subscriptions and payments.
  • Supabase: For database and user management.

6. Your Rights

  • You can view, update, or delete your account at any time in the settings panel.
  • You may request access to all data we hold about you via email.
  • You can opt-out of non-essential emails.

7. Compliance & Contact

Outflux complies with privacy laws including GDPR where applicable. For questions or concerns about data handling, please contact us at support@outflux.ai.

Add a Campaign and Set It Up

Begin by creating a new campaign. Give it a meaningful name and configure its status â€” you can pause or activate the campaign at any time. This forms the foundation of your email outreach.

Next, create a recipient list for your campaign. You can manually add contacts or import them via CSV. Organizing your audience into lists helps you target your emails effectively.

3
Create the Email Content

Compose the email message for your campaign. Drag and drop the blocks from the left and style them using the tab on the right to create the perfect email.

4
Select the List and Pick the Date

Assign your email to the appropriate recipient list and schedule the sending date and time. You can also choose to send immediately if desired. The scheduling system allows precise control over your campaign timeline.

5

This will be the email your emails will be sent from. Navigate to settings > connect then select google. This will take you to google where you will sign in and allow permissions for outflux to send emails from your behalf.
